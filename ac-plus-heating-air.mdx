### Common problems an AC Plus Heating & Air service call addresses

Extreme heat accelerates wear. That fact shapes every AC Plus Heating & Air service call in the High Desert, where compressors, capacitors, and refrigerant lines face conditions more demanding than those in milder regions. The Victorville climate pushes residential systems to run longer daily cycles, which shortens component life and raises the frequency of mid-season breakdowns. Catching a failing capacitor or a low refrigerant charge early prevents a full compressor replacement, which is one of the most expensive repairs in residential HVAC.

The problems below represent the most frequent reasons customers schedule an AC Plus Heating & Air service call in the High Desert.

- **Refrigerant loss:** Refrigerant loss causes the system to blow warm air and forces the compressor to work harder, shortening its life.

- **Capacitor failure:** Capacitor failure prevents the compressor or fan motor from starting, which stops cooling entirely.

- **Dirty coils:** Dirty coils reduce heat transfer efficiency, raising energy use and indoor temperatures simultaneously.

- **Clogged drain lines:** Clogged drain lines back up condensate into the air handler, risking water damage and mold growth.

- **Thermostat faults:** Thermostat faults send incorrect signals to the system, causing short cycling or failure to reach the set temperature.

- **Duct leaks:** Duct leaks bleed conditioned air into unconditioned spaces, cutting delivery efficiency and raising utility bills.
